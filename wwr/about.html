History

Splash White Water Rafting was founded in 2010 by a group of adventure enthusiasts who shared a deep passion for whitewater rafting and a commitment to preserving natural waterways. Starting as a small, family-operated business offering guided rafting tours on the nearby rivers, SPLASH quickly gained a reputation for thrilling, safe, and eco-conscious adventures.

Over the years, the company expanded its operations, exploring more rivers and introducing innovative experiences such as moonlit rafting and multi-day excursions. Today, Splash remains dedicated to its roots, blending heart-pounding excitement with a focus on sustainability, community, and respect for nature.
