Let's Plan Your Trip

Discover Our Rafting Adventures

After the day's aquatic excitement, unwind at a secluded riverside campsite. Here, you'll set up tents, gather around a campfire, and share stories under a canopy of stars. The campsite offers basic amenities, allowing you to immerse yourself in the natural surroundings while enjoying the camaraderie of fellow adventurers.

The adventure continues with guided hikes through picturesque trails that wind along the river and ascend into the surrounding hills. These hikes offer a chance to explore diverse ecosystems, observe local wildlife, and capture breathtaking vistas. Trails vary in difficulty, accommodating both novice and experienced hikers.

Prepare to navigate Class IV and V rapids, characterized by powerful currents, steep drops, and challenging obstacles. These sections demand quick reflexes and precise teamwork to maneuver through the tumultuous waters. Participants should be in good physical condition and possess prior rafting experience to fully enjoy this segment.
